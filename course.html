Electric Vehicle Mastery Program

This course provides a comprehensive overview of electric vehicle technology and the automotive industry. You will gain hands-on experience in designing, developing, and optimizing EV powertrains. Through in-depth study of battery systems, power electronics, and motor control, you will develop the skills necessary to excel in the emerging EV market. This program combines technical expertise with career preparation, equipping you for success in the automotive industry.

Course Schedule: Weekdays: 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM | Saturday: External Mentorship & Project Work

Learning Outcomes

  • Master the fundamentals of electric vehicle technology and the automotive industry.
  • Develop expertise in EV powertrain design and development, including modeling, simulation, and component selection.
  • Acquire practical skills in battery management, power electronics, and electric machine control for optimal EV performance and efficiency.
  • Prepare for a successful career in the EV industry through hands-on projects, industry-relevant training, and career development support.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree (B.Tech) or Master's degree (M.Tech) in:
  • Electrical Engineering (EEE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Allied Engineering branches (related to Electrical, Electronics, or Mechanical Engineering)

This Course Includes

  • Real-time project work (Weeks 5 to 12)
  • Resume Preparation & Mock Interviews (Week 13)
  • Group Discussion & Individual Presentation (Week 14)
  • Final Project Demo (Week 15)
  • Final Placement Drives (Weeks 15 to 16)
