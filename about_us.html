About us

From idea to impact

At EV Academy, we are dedicated to transforming the future of the Electric Vehicle (EV) industry. EV Academy is not just an educational institution; it's a catalyst for change. We are committed to bridging the gap between academia and industry, providing students with the practical skills and knowledge required to excel in the EV sector. Our programs are designed to foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and a deep understanding of the EV ecosystem.

Mission

Become the worldwide leader in Electric Vehicle Design & Manufacturing by 2035!

Vision

Empower India’s talent to shape the global EV industry for the next 100 years!
